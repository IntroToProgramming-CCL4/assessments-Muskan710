About us

Efficiency, Building, Budget & Time

Bison Contracting L.L.C provides a wide range of services to its customers Ranging from Industrial/ Commercial Building Construction to Design & Built Turnkey Interior Fit out. Our goal is simple and well defined: provide outstanding value by consistently producing excellent results and giving clients the maximum return on their investment & maximum productivity. Projects are approached at the outset by gaining a thorough understanding of client needs and budget parameters. When work begins, the keywords that underlie the construction process are "innovation" and "efficiency". Jobs are carefully orchestrated, with special attention always being paid to completion schedules, code adherence and, of course, quality control. At a time when building industry has never been more expensive, BISON came with the goal and singular purpose of creating quality projects of exceptional value. For the success of the project , we ensure a seamless work progression which is charted out in our Project management procedure and activity schedule. Our proactive competent team guarantees on time delivery of the highest quality service and product.

Our Mission

To help organizations reap the benefits of sustainability for Offices & residences through world-class environmental ratings, by enhancing operational efficiency and cultivating healthier, happier spaces keeping our valuable clients interest in mind.

Our Vision

A world where every space exists in symphony with nature and effortlessly resonates with our environment. A total solution provider our capabilities encompass virtually all aspects of a project including sustainability and resource conservation. Though we enrich all our projects with sound environmental and energy efficiency principles, what sets us apart is our ability to execute sustainable interiors and assist our clients with best environment available .
